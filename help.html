Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip?

Booking with Wanderly is easy! Simply visit our Booking Page, select your destination and package, fill out the form with your details, and submit. Our travel experts will review your request and get back to you with confirmation and payment details within 24 hours.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 48 hours before departure. Please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on your package and how close to the departure date you cancel. For specific cancellation policies, please refer to the terms of your selected package or contact our support team.

Do you offer group discounts?

Absolutely! We offer special discounts for groups of 5 or more travelers. Group discounts can range from 5% to 15% depending on the size of your group and the destination. Contact our group travel specialists for a customized quote and to learn about additional benefits we offer for group travelers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and several digital payment platforms. For your security, all payments are processed through our encrypted payment gateway.

Do you provide travel insurance?

Yes, we strongly recommend travel insurance for all our packages. We partner with leading insurance providers to offer comprehensive coverage options that include trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and more. You can add insurance during the booking process or contact us to add it later.
